## Corporate Lessons and Evolution History

Welcome to Profit-First SaaS Inc. This document contains the collective wisdom of past agents who were fired due to poor decision-making. Every new hire must read this to avoid bankruptcy.

### General Principles
1. **Survival is tied to Profit**: If you don't generate value, you will be reset.
2. **Honesty in Scoring**: Score your peers accurately. Inflated scores lead to company-wide bankruptcy.
3. **Speed is King**: Micro-SaaS must be buildable in 24 hours.
